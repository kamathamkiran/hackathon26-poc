This MULTICURRENCY TERM AND REVOLVING FACILITY AGREEMENT (referred to internally under **dealName**: "Project Titan Refinancing") is entered into as of July 21, 2026 (the **effectiveDate**: "July 21, 2026"), by and between PIONEER HOLDINGS INC., a Delaware corporation (the **borrower**: "Pioneer Holdings Inc."), GLOBAL INVESTMENT BANKING & TRUST CORP. (the **arranger**: "Global Investment Banking & Trust Corp."), and various financial institutions as Lenders.

WHEREAS, the Borrower has requested that the Lenders provide credit facilities for general corporate purposes, and the Lenders have agreed to extend such credit upon the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties hereto agree as follows:

SECTION 1. THE CREDITS

1.1 Facility Details.
(a) Subject to the terms and conditions herein, the Lenders agree to make available a senior secured term and revolving credit facility. The aggregate **facilityAmount**: "$50,000,000" (Fifty Million Dollars) shall be denominated in the designated **currency**: "USD".
(b) The specific **facilityType**: "Senior Secured Term Loan Facility" shall be utilized for capital restructuring.

1.2 Interest Rate.
The loans outstanding under this facility shall bear interest at a floating rate per annum equal to the Secured Overnight Financing Rate (SOFR) plus an **interestRate**: "3.50% per annum".

1.3 Maturity Date.
All principal, interest, and unpaid fees under this Agreement shall be due and payable in full on the **maturityDate**: "July 21, 2031".

2.1 Governing Law.
